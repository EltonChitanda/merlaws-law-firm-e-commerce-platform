## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Navigate to Registration:**
   - Go to the MerLaws website
   - Click "Login" or "Client Portal"
   - Click "Register" or "Create Account"

2. **Fill in Registration Form:**
   - **Full Name:** Enter your full legal name
   - **Email Address:** Use a valid email you check regularly
   - **Password:** Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password
   - **Phone Number:** (Optional) Your contact number
   - **Newsletter:** (Optional) Subscribe to updates
   - **Terms of Service:** Check the box to accept terms

3. **Submit Registration:**
   - Click "Register" or "Create Account"
   - You'll be redirected to the login page upon success

### Logging In

1. **Access Login Page:**
   - Go to the MerLaws website
   - Click "Login" or "Client Portal"

2. **Enter Credentials:**
   - **Email:** Your registered email address
   - **Password:** Your account password
   - **Remember Me:** (Optional) Stay logged in

3. **Login:**
   - Click "Login" or "Sign In"
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the reset link in the email
5. Enter your new password
6. Log in with your new password

## Case Management

### Creating a New Case

1. **Navigate to Cases:**
   - Click "Cases" in the main menu
   - Click "Create New Case" button

2. **Fill in Case Details:**
   - **Case Title:** Brief description of your case
   - **Case Type:** Select from dropdown (Medical Negligence, MVA, etc.)
   - **Description:** Detailed description of your case
   - **Priority:** Select priority level (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)

3. **Submit Case:**
   - Click "Create Case" or "Submit"
   - Your case will be created and assigned a case number
   - You'll receive a confirmation notification

### Viewing Your Cases

1. **Access Cases List:**
   - Click "Cases" in the main menu
   - View all your cases in a list

2. **Case Information Displayed:**
   - Case number and title
   - Case type
   - Status (Draft, Active, Under Review, Closed)
   - Priority level
   - Last updated date
   - Document count
   - Service request count

3. **Filtering and Sorting:**
   - Filter by status
   - Sort by date, priority, or status
   - Search by case title or number

### Viewing Case Details

1. **Open a Case:**
   - Click on a case from the cases list
   - View complete case information

2. **Case Details Page Shows:**
   - Full case description
   - Case status and priority
   - Assigned attorney (if assigned)
   - Case timeline and activities
   - Associated documents
   - Service requests
   - Messages related to the case
   - Invoices (if any)

### Editing a Case

1. **Access Edit:**
   - Open the case you want to edit
   - Click "Edit Case" button

2. **Make Changes:**
   - Update case title, description, or priority
   - Note: Some fields may be restricted based on case status

3. **Save Changes:**
   - Click "Save" or "Update Case"
   - Changes will be saved and logged

### Case Statuses

- **Draft:** Case created but not yet submitted/activated
- **Active:** Case is active and being worked on
- **Under Review:** Case is under review by attorneys
- **Closed:** Case has been completed or closed

## Document Management

### Uploading Documents

1. **Access Upload:**
   - Open the case you want to upload documents to
   - Click "Upload Document" button
   - Or go to "Documents" section and click "Upload"

2. **Select File:**
   - Click "Choose File" or "Browse"
   - Select the file from your computer
   - **Allowed formats:** PDF, JPEG, JPG
   - **Maximum size:** 100MB

3. **Add Document Information:**
   - **Document Type:** (Optional) Select document type
   - **Description:** (Optional) Add description or notes

4. **Upload:**
   - Click "Upload" or "Submit"
   - Wait for upload to complete
   - You'll see a success message

### Viewing Documents

1. **Access Documents:**
   - Open a case
   - Go to "Documents" section
   - Or click "Documents" in the main menu

2. **Document List Shows:**
   - Document name
   - Upload date
   - File size
   - Uploaded by
   - Document type (if specified)

3. **Download Documents:**
   - Click on a document name
   - Document will download to your computer

### Document Requirements

- **File Types:** PDF, JPEG, JPG only
- **File Size:** Maximum 100MB per file
- **Naming:** Use descriptive filenames
- **Security:** Documents are securely stored and encrypted

## Service Requests

### Understanding Service Requests

Service requests allow you to request additional services for your case, such as:
- Consultations
- Doctor visits
- Transportation
- Document reviews
- Other legal services

### Adding Services to Cart

1. **Access Service Catalog:**
   - Open a case
   - Click "Request Services" or "Service Catalog"
   - Or go to "Services" in the main menu

2. **Browse Services:**
   - View available services by category
   - Read service descriptions
   - Check estimated duration and details

3. **Add to Cart:**
   - Click "Add to Cart" on desired services
   - For consultations, you may need to specify:
     - Consultation date
     - Consultation time
     - Location preference

4. **View Cart:**
   - Click "View Cart" or cart icon
   - Review selected services
   - Remove items if needed

### Submitting Service Requests

1. **Review Cart:**
   - Ensure all desired services are in cart
   - Add any notes or special requests

2. **Submit for Approval:**
   - Click "Submit for Approval"
   - Service requests will be sent to admin for review
   - You'll receive a confirmation notification

3. **Request Status:**
   - **Cart:** Still in your cart (not submitted)
   - **Pending:** Submitted, awaiting admin approval
   - **Approved:** Approved by admin, will be scheduled
   - **Rejected:** Request was declined (check admin notes)

### Tracking Service Requests

- View request status in case details
- Receive notifications when status changes
- Check admin notes for additional information

## Messaging

### Sending Messages

1. **Access Messaging:**
   - Click "Messages" in the main menu
   - Or open a case and go to "Messages" section

2. **Compose Message:**
   - Click "Compose" or "New Message"
   - **To:** Select recipient (usually your attorney)
   - **Subject:** Enter message subject
   - **Case:** (Optional) Link to specific case
   - **Message:** Type your message

3. **Send:**
   - Click "Send"
   - Message will be delivered
   - You'll see it in your sent messages

### Viewing Messages

1. **Message List:**
   - Go to "Messages" in main menu
   - View all message threads

2. **Message Threads:**
   - Messages are organized by conversation
   - Click on a thread to view messages
   - Unread messages are highlighted

3. **Replying to Messages:**
   - Open a message thread
   - Type your reply
   - Click "Reply" or "Send"

### Message Features

- **Threaded Conversations:** Messages are grouped by conversation
- **Case Linking:** Link messages to specific cases
- **Read Receipts:** See when messages are read
- **Attachments:** (If enabled) Attach files to messages

## Payments and Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

1. **Access Invoices:**
   - Click "Invoices" or "Payments" in main menu

2. **Invoice Information:**
   - Invoice number
   - Amount due
   - Due date
   - Status (Sent, Paid, Overdue)

### Paying an Invoice

1. **Select Invoice:**
   - Click on an unpaid invoice

2. **Pay Now:**
   - Click "Pay Now" button

3. **Complete Payment:**
   - Enter payment details on PayFast
   - Complete payment

   - Payment confirmation will be shown

### Payment Methods

- **PayFast Online Payment:** Credit card, debit card, EFT
- **Bank Transfer:** (If arranged with admin)
- **Other methods:** Contact billing department

### Payment History

- View all past payments
- Download payment receipts
- View payment status

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Cannot Log In

**Problem:** Cannot log in with correct credentials

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email and password are correct
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Try password reset
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser
6. Contact support if issue persists

#### File Upload Fails

**Problem:** Cannot upload documents

**Solutions:**
1. Check file format (PDF, JPEG, JPG only)
2. Check file size (maximum 100MB)
3. Check internet connection
4. Try smaller file size
5. Try different file format
6. Contact support if issue persists

#### Cannot View Case

**Problem:** Case not showing or access denied

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're logged in
2. Check if case belongs to you
3. Refresh the page
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### Payment Issues

**Problem:** Payment not processing

**Solutions:**
1. Check payment details
2. Verify internet connection
3. Try different payment method
4. Contact PayFast support
5. Contact billing department

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I have multiple cases?

**A:** Yes, you can create and manage multiple cases. Each case is independent.

### Q: How do I know if my case is being worked on?

**A:** Check your case status and activity timeline. You'll also receive notifications for updates.

### Q: Can I delete a case?

**A:** Cases cannot be deleted by clients. Contact your attorney if you need to close a case.

### Q: How long are documents stored?

**A:** Documents are stored securely for the duration of your case and as required by law.

### Q: What if I need to change my email?

**A:** Contact support to change your email address. Email verification may be required.

### Q: Can I access the system from mobile devices?

**A:** Yes, the system is mobile-responsive and can be accessed from smartphones and tablets.
